Entrepreneurial activity and Internships

Caulys

Since January 2018

Foundation of a start-up

Caulys aims to reinsert agriculture in cities. Thanks to sophisticated climate control and the use of alternative plants' growing methods, anyone could grow several fruits and vegetables, all year long, directly at home. I have lauched this start-up in mid-2018 ; we since then have beed supported by Innosuisse, XGrant, Laforge, Genilem, EIT Food and MassChallenge leading to encouraging results. Our first greenhouses are installed at EPFL campus !

Alternative Energies and Atomic Energy Comission (CEA)

July 2016 - December 2016

Internship in R&D

Created by General de Gaulle after the World War two, the CEA had as first aim to make researches concerning nuclear utilisation, for energy, defense and technology. Nowadays, CEA's activities are widely broadened, and this research center is nowadays placed as the world leader in tele-operation systems. I have worked with Philippe Garrec to developp a haptic mechanical hand, in a view to one day replace glove boxes. This research and developpment experience learned me how to manipulate very precise mechanical systems, to design a new efficient exoskeleton prototype. After having discovered a new mechanical system, I wrote a patent specification that has been approved by CEA. Maybe this will lead to future patent application.
